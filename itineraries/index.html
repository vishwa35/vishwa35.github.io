Itineraries

NYC -> Acadia#

    Acadia Highlights Trails Along the Way
    • Cliffs along Ocean Path
      • Trail or drive along the coast with parking lots and scenic stops
      • Highlights: Otter Cliffs and Thunder Hole
    • Sand Beach
      • Nice and cold water, north side of Ocean Path starts here
      • Note: Northmost part of the trail is less exciting
    • Cadillac Mountain
      • Highest peak, hike or drive up
      • Sunrise is beautiful, sometimes the first in the US to see it
      • *Permit required during summer to drive up*https://recreation.gov
    • Jordan Pond
      • Large pond surrounded by mountains
      • Teahouse/cafe open in summer, might need a reservation
      • Easy trail around the pond, connects to other trails
    • Eagle Lake and Echo Lake
      • Internal water viewpoints, less time spent here
    • Bubbles Trail
      • Moderate to hard, rock scrambling
      • Balanced rock and summit of North Bubble
    • Jordan Pond Loop
      • Easy, half boardwalk and half dirt path
    • Beehive Trail
      • Steep ascent, iron rings to help climb
      • Great views of the Atlantic, comes down via the Bowl Trail
    • The Bowl Trail
      • More chill way to the top, pond at the top
    • Gorham Mountain Trail
      • Good views of Otter Cliffs and the ocean
    • Ocean Path Trail
      • Easy, walking along the coastline, peaceful and good views
    • Combined Trails (Including Above)
      • Beehive, The Bowl, Gorham Mountain, Ocean Drive Loop
      • Bubbles Divide via Jordans Pond Path
    • New Haven, CT
      • Stopped for coffee, Yale University nearby
    • Mystic, CT
      • Small town, easy to walk around, shops and ice cream
    • Providence, RI
      • Nice downtown and waterfront, beautiful state library
      • Symposium books and boba, vegan donuts and food hall
    • Newport, RI
      • Cliff Walk and mansions
    • Portsmouth, NH
      • Fun to walk around, good food stop
      • Green Elephant vegetarian restaurant and smoothies at The Green Room
    • Portland, ME
      • Eat at Green Elephant, walk around main area and old port
      • Lighthouses nearby
    • Freeport, ME
      • Outlets for outdoor gear stores, nice place to stretch legs
    • Bangor, ME
      • Surprisingly nice town, Chimera Coffee and main area walk

    • Inamo - Japanese food

    • Getting Around
      • Get an oyster card, the tubegets you around central london great. Figure out how often you're gonna take it though, verywalkable city and since you're visiting you wanna walk + see the area so maybe not worth week pass etc. Oyster card has fare capping so you'll only get charged max some amount a day, week, etc.
